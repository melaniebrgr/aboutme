More about me

I like to create, particularly at the intersection of art, science, and technology. I started digital painting during a MSc in Chemistry, and during a subsequent MSc in Biomedical Communication I rediscovered web development. Things have gotten more difficult since GeoCities.

At present, I can be found at Imagineeringart during the week, where I illustrate science textbooks. On weekends I enjoy spending time with my boyfriend, Vince, and my quadruped nephew, Pedro. I also organize monthly events on behalf of the BMCAA. Sometimes I tweet, write, code pens, paint, and speak. I live in Toronto, Canada, home of Drake.
